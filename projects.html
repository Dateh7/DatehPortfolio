EazyCuts — Barber Booking System

A full-stack barber shop management system built using Spring Boot, Thymeleaf, and MySQL. It handles 30-minute appointment slots, QR-based walk-ins, email notifications, and automated revenue summaries.

  • Customers book or walk in through a simple UI.
  • Admins view daily schedules and mark appointments as completed or no-show.
  • Revenue tracking dashboard calculates totals per service type.
  • System generates weekly booking slots automatically.
